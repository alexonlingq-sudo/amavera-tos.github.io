What Amavera Does

Amavera is a mobile application that helps users document the condition of a residential property using AI-assisted photo capture and analysis. The app generates a structured inspection report based on photos taken by the user during a walkthrough of the property.

Amavera is a documentation tool. It records and describes what is visible in the photos you provide. It does not perform a professional property inspection, and it does not replace one.

AI-Generated Content

Amavera's inspection reports are generated by artificial intelligence. AI analysis is based on what is visible in the photos you submit. The AI may:

  • Misidentify rooms, fixtures, or surfaces
  • Fail to detect damage or conditions that are present in a photo
  • Describe conditions inaccurately
  • Assign confidence levels that do not reflect the actual completeness of the inspection

You should review every report for accuracy. Amavera presents its findings with transparency, including confidence scores and explicit statements of limitation, but the AI is not infallible. The report reflects the AI's best interpretation of the photos provided — it is not a statement of fact.

Reports and Immutability

Once a report is finalized, it cannot be altered. The finalized report is timestamped and may carry a cryptographic hash to verify that its contents have not changed since generation. This immutability is a feature designed to preserve the integrity of the document. It also means that errors in a finalized report cannot be corrected — you would need to conduct a new inspection.
